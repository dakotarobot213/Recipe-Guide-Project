Fluffy Pancake Recipe

This recipe makes it easy to make delicous fluffy pancakes

Random fun fact, Shakespeare apparently loved pancakes so much they often mentioned them in plays.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of all-purpose or plain flour (280g | 10 oz)
  • 1/4 cup of granulated sugar or sweetener, (60g | 2 oz)
  • 4 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups milk (440ml)
  • 1/4 cup butter (60g | 2 oz)
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg

Instructions:

  1. Combine the flour, sugar (or sweetener), baking powder, baking soda and salt together within a large-sized bowl.
  2. Make a well in the centre and add the milk, slightly cooled melted butter, vanilla and egg.
  3. Wisk the mixture together before slowly folding them into the dry ingredients. Mix together until smooth it's (there may be a couple of lumps but that's okay).
  4. Set the batter aside and allow to rest while heating up your pan or griddle.
  5. Heat a nonstick pan or griddle over low-medium heat and wipe over with a little butter to lightly grease pan.
  6. Pour Â¼ cup of batter onto the pan and spread out gently into a round shape with the back of your ladle or measuring cup.
  7. When the underside is golden and bubbles begin to appear on the surface, flip with a spatula and cook until golden. Repeat with remaining batter.
